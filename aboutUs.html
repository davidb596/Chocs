ABOUT US

Our story began in 2003 where - thanks to the creative spirit of our chocolate chef David Bonardo Purba and Matthew Georgiano Karnadi - the CHOCS saw the light of day! A delicious chunk of chocolate on a wooden spoon, with which the tastiest cup of hot chocolate can be made. Our CHOCS is now available in almost every convenience store and now we have expanded our CHOCS range with artisan bonbons, cool choc bars, cheerful chocolate fondues and much more. Our chocolate chefs really "cook" with chocolate and use premium cocoa beans and the best spices and other ingredients. The result: the most surprising and special flavor combinations that you can't get enough of!
